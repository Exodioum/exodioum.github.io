5 Hairstyles!

1 Half-Up Twist

This style is remarkably simple—a couple of twists, a few pins, and you’re done—and yet it’s undeniably striking.

2 Braid Halo

This is the perfect hairstyle for girls with short hair. And the best thing is that it only costs you five minutes of your precious time!

3 Chill Waves

The perfect hairstyle for everyone who wants to impress, but doesn't want to stand hours in front of the mirror. Grab a straightener and you are ready in a jiffy.

4 Sleek af Pony

To late for a night out and your boyfriend is already in the door opening to pick you up? No stress! The Sleek af Pony never lets you down.

5 To the Fringes

The best hairstyle, especially for curly hair. But it also does the trick with straight hair as well. For special occasions, but also a very good alternative to a boring everyday hairstyle.

Be healthy!

1 Drink more water

You definetely do not want to be dehydrated, make sure to drink a glass of water at least every 2 hours. You can also carry a bottle of water to school.

2 Regular sleep pattern

For the average teen it is important to have at least 8 hours of sleep a night. The amount of hours you sleep a night really affects your health. Get enough sleep for better alertness during the day and less anxiety.

3 Eat breakfast everyday

Breakfast is the most important meal of the day, because it gives you the necessary energy you need for the beginning of your day. Good breakfast foods include milk, eggs, fruit or toast.

4 Keep good posture

Hold yourself straight. This might sound like an unnecessary option but trust me, it is! Holding a good posture during the day will make you feel more energetic.

5 Eat healthy food

Replace the unhealthy food with healthy food. You can replace white bread with brown bread. Avoid sweets and eat fruit!
